SERVER RULES

To ensure fair play and enjoyable experience for everyone, please follow these rules.

General Rules

  • No cheating, hacking, or exploiting game bugs
  • No racism, sexism, or hate speech of any kind
  • No spawn camping or excessive team killing
  • Respect all players and admins
  • No inappropriate usernames or clan tags

Gameplay Rules

  • No camping in restricted areas (map specific)
  • No glitching outside of map boundaries
  • No excessive use of overpowered weapons
  • Follow game mode specific rules
  • Admins have final say in rule interpretations

Violation Penalties

Breaking any of these rules may result in warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans depending on severity. Repeated offenses will lead to increasingly severe punishments.
